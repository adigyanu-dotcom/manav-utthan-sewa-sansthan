Welcome!

Welcome to Manav Utthan Seva Sansthan (Human Uplift Service Organization)

Manav Utthan Seva Sansthan (MUSS) is a dedicated non-profit organization committed to the holistic development and empowerment of communities.

Registered under Societies Registration Act 21, 1860, we operate with a mission to drive social change and uplift the underprivileged through targeted initiatives and sustainable programs.
We were Established on 26th of May 2014, Registration Number 88/14-05.

Our Services

MUS plays a vital role in maintaining the cleanliness and safety of our municipalities. As an active participant in the Swachh Bharat Abhiyan, we are committed to creating cleaner, healthier communities through efficient waste management and sanitation solutions.

Our Services

We offer a comprehensive range of municipal support services, including:

Door-to-Door Waste Collection & Management
– Conducted in full compliance with municipal guidelines to ensure hygienic waste disposal.

Supply of Sanitation Materials
– Provision of essential cleaning agents such as industrial limestone and bleaching powder.

Provision of Cleaning Equipment
– Supply of tools and utilities like brooms, compactors, waste bins, and containers.

Garbage Transportation
– Reliable transportation of waste using dedicated trucks and tractors.

Our Mission

Beyond sanitation, MUS is deeply committed to rural development and social upliftment. Our workforce primarily consists of individuals from underprivileged communities, offering them meaningful employment opportunities and a path to a better livelihood.

Activities of the Institution

MUS is the major contributor in keeping our municipality safe and clean. We are one of the key member of Swaksh Bharat Abhiyan and are dedicated to make sure our Municipalities are clean.

Our Services include:

1. Door to Door Waste Collection and Management as per Municipality Guidelines
2. Supply of Proper Waste and Cleaning Materials like Industrial Lime Stone and Bleaching Powder
3. Supply of Cleaning utilities like Broom, compactors, and containers
4. Garbage transportation using trucks and tractors

We are dedicated in providing and uplifting of rural development of the poor section of society. Our entire team consists of underprivileged people, who can benefit from the getting a proper employment.

Become a Member & Fee Structure

Manav Utthan Seva Sansthan welcomes individuals who are passionate about social change to join our mission. Becoming a member allows you to actively participate in our initiatives and contribute to the community's upliftment.

Membership Fee Details

The organization has a straightforward and accessible membership fee structure, designed to encourage widespread participation. The details, as referenced in our documents, are as follows:

  • General Membership Fee: The fee for becoming a member is ₹ 25/ (Twenty-Five Rupees Only).
  • Annual Subscription: In addition to the one-time membership fee, there is a modest annual subscription of ₹ 3/ (Three Rupees Only) to maintain active membership status.

How to Become a Member?

  1. Filling out an Application Form: Providing your personal details and expressing your interest in joining.
  2. Payment of Fees: Submitting the one-time membership fee of ₹ 25/ along with the annual subscription.
  3. Approval Process: The application is then reviewed and approved by the governing body of the Sansthan as per its bylaws.

Upon successful enrollment, members are formally recognized and can participate in the organization's activities and governance, as outlined in our rules and regulations.

Our Legal Framework

Manav Utthan Seva Sansthan is a formally recognized entity, duly registered under the following statutes, which govern our operations and ensure transparency and accountability:

1. Societies Registration Act 21, 1860: This is our primary registration, establishing us as a formal non-profit society.
2. Indian Stamp Act: This registration authorizes us to deal in non-judicial stamp papers and other related fiscal documents.
